📄 Replit AI Build Brief – BeehiveStack Deal Desk Integration

Project Name: BeehiveStack Deal Desk

Goal: Add dual onboarding flows (Investors + Sellers) into the live BeehiveStack site, store structured data, and generate anonymized “deal cards” for matchmaking.

🔑 Features to Implement
1. Investor Onboarding Flow

Add a new landing page section: “Investor Network”

Embed signup form (name, email → ConvertKit tag Investor – Pending Onboarding).

After signup → redirect to Onboarding Form page.

Investor Onboarding Form Fields:

Name, Email, Phone, LinkedIn (optional)

Investment Budget Range (dropdown brackets)

Preferred Investment Structure (acquisition / equity / partnership)

Business Types (multi-select: SaaS, eCom, affiliate, digital products, content/media)

Target Monthly Revenue (MRR/profit range)

Deal Size Comfort Zone (multiples)

Timeline to Invest (immediate, 1–3 mo, 3–6 mo, later)

Desired Level of Involvement (hands-off / partial / active)

Checkbox: “Keep my identity confidential until I approve a deal.”

Checkbox: “Interested in premium deal flow (paid).”

→ Data should be stored in Airtable (Investors table) with unique Investor ID.
→ ConvertKit tag changes from Pending → Investor – Active.

2. Seller Onboarding Flow

Add a new landing page section: “Sell Your Website”

Embed signup form (name, email → ConvertKit tag Seller – Pending Onboarding).

After signup → redirect to Onboarding Form page.

Seller Onboarding Form Fields:

Name, Email, Phone, LinkedIn (optional)

Website URL(s)

Business Type (SaaS, eCom, Affiliate, Digital, Content/Media)

Monetization Methods (subs, ads, affiliate, product sales, sponsorships)

Current MRR / Profit (bracketed)

Revenue Trend (growing / flat / declining)

Avg. Monthly Traffic + Top 3 Sources

Operational Load (hours per week)

Team Size

Exit Timeline (immediate / 1–3mo / 3–6mo / later)

Valuation Expectation (open field)

Willing to Consider (full sale / partial / partnership)

Checkbox: “Keep my site anonymized until I approve investor intros.”

Checkbox: “Interested in Fast Track seller package (priority listing).”

Optional File Upload (P&L, GA, Stripe screenshot).

→ Data stored in Airtable (Sellers table) with unique Seller ID.
→ ConvertKit tag changes from Pending → Seller – Active.

3. Deal Cards Generator (Anonymized Listings)

Create an internal tool or script that pulls fields from Airtable and auto-generates a deal card format:

Deal Card Example (Investor Digest):

Business Type: SaaS

Monthly Revenue: $2.5K

Monetization: Subscriptions

Traffic: 10K/mo (SEO + Direct)

Workload: 8 hrs/week

Age: 3 years

Asking Multiple: 36x

Status: Available
(Anonymized — no seller name/URL until NDA signed)

Export Deal Digest monthly as HTML email (ConvertKit campaign).

4. Automation & Integration

Zapier/Make automation:

Form submission → Airtable record created → ConvertKit tag update.

Airtable filter → Generate anonymized deal cards → Send via ConvertKit.

Ensure anonymity until deal request is approved.

NDA trigger workflow: when an investor clicks “Request Full Details,” automatically send NDA and unlock seller info.

🎨 UI/UX Notes

Match BeehiveStack branding (modern clean tech, trust-driven design).

Two clear CTAs:

“Invest in Digital Assets”

“Sell Your Website”

Keep process frictionless: Signup → Form → Thank You page.

🛠 Tech Stack & Requirements

Frontend: Existing BeehiveStack live site (integrate new sections/pages).

Backend: Airtable (Investors, Sellers, Deals tables).

Email/Automation: ConvertKit.

Automation: Zapier/Make.

Hosting: Continue with live stack (Replit-deployed).

✅ Deliverable: A fully working dual intake system (investors + sellers) with Airtable storage, ConvertKit tagging, and anonymized deal digest generator — live on BeehiveStack.
